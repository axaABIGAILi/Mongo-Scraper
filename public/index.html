What is Tokusatsu?

The Tokusatsu genre refers to live-action film that uses a heavy amount of special effects. Common themes in the genre are horror, fantasy, or sci-fi. A few notable standouts in the genre include Godzilla, Kamen Rider and Power Rangers!
